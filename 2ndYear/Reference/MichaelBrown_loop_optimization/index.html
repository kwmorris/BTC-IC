Control Loop Optimization tutorials

Back to main index

These tutorials were freely provided by Michael Brown, a process control engineer based in South Africa, for educational use. Mr. Brown has a wealth of practical knowledge and experience to share on the subject of control loop tuning and optimization. One would do well to heed his many pieces of advice on how to wring the best performance out of PID-controlled loops!.

The Case Histories in particular are wonderful, showcasing real examples of poor control, faulty instrumentation, unworkable control strategies, and other process control blunders encountered in Mr. Brown's many years of consulting as a process control engineer. Most importantly, these Case Histories demonstrate how the problems were found and what was done to correct them, not just what the problems were. A virtual gold-mine of learning for those interested in becoming proficient at optimizing automatic control systems.
